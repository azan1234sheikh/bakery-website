The Jibarito Sandwich

THE BEST SANDWITCH YOU HAVE EVER TASTED!

Jibarito-sandwich Recipe

The Jibarito sandwich, a Puerto Rican dish, replaces bread with fried green plantains and typically includes steak,
garlic mayo, lettuce, tomato, and cheese. Here’s how you can make it

Ingredients:

2 large green plantains
Vegetable oil for frying
Salt to taste
For the Steak Filling:
1 lb steak
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup mayonnaise
Instructions:
Prepare the Plantains:
Peel the plantains and cut them in half lengthwise.
Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
Fry the plantain halves until they are golden, about 2-3 minutes per side.
Remove the plantains and drain on paper towels.
Flatten the fried plantain halves using a flat surface or the bottom of a heavy pan.
Return the flattened plantains to the oil and fry until crispy, another 2-3 minutes per side.
Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
PREP 15 mins
COOK 15 mins
READY IN 50 mins
